Job search from the command line

Stop clicking your way through job boards. Get the raw data right to your command line, thengrep, cut or fzf job postings however you like.

It's free. No sign-in required.

pip install jobcli

  • About
  • Job search sends developers back into the stone age by forcing us to scroll and click through a bunch of websites like regular folks.JobCLI was created to deliver job postings to where we are most comfortable: the terminal.
